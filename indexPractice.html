"Made from scratch,
Brewed with passion"

At our core, we aim to create a welcoming space where guests can enjoy the perfect balance of amazing food made with quality ingredients, and exceptional craft beer while standing out from the rest. Whether it’s indulging in our hand-crafted pizzas or savoring our imaginative culinary offerings, we pride ourselves on delivering outstanding service in an inviting atmosphere, ideal for sharing with friends, family, coworkers, and the community. As proud members of the Tucson beer scene, we’re dedicated to representing our vibrant community, not just to locals but to the many travelers who visit our city. Our flagship brews, always on tap, showcase the best Tucson has to offer with a diverse selection including two IPAs, a Blonde, an Amber, two Stouts, a Hefeweizen, and our beloved Green Chili Ale. To keep things fresh, we also feature a rotating variety of seasonal beers throughout the year, ensuring there's always something new and exciting to enjoy.
